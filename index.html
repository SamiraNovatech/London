London-The Capital of the UK

Iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, the Tower of London, and Buckingham Palace reflect centuries of heritage, while the Shard and Canary Wharf showcase its global financial power. The River Thames flows through the heart of the city, connecting diverse neighborhoods rich in culture and tradition. London is renowned for its museums, theaters, and bustling markets, offering endless opportunities for exploration.
